Why this works Why the cat thinks this works

The agent does not predict football better than the market. It converts market odds into score probabilities and selects the result with the highest expected Kicktipp points. The cat does not claim to see the future. It simply sniffs the market odds, converts them into score probabilities and chooses the bowl with the most expected tuna.

Parameter glossary Cat dictionary

Agent Pick Cat Pick
Exact score recommended for Kicktipp — chosen to maximize expected points, not to mirror the most likely final result. Exact score the cat slides into your bowl — chosen for maximum expected tuna, not because it is the most likely nap outcome.
Expected Value (EV) Tuna Value
Average Kicktipp points this tip earns across all possible match outcomes (2 / 3 / 4 point rules). Average tuna points this score earns across all possible nap outcomes (2 / 3 / 4 treat rules).
Market Probability (1 / X / 2) Nap Probability (1 / X / 2)
De-vigged implied win / draw / away probabilities from bookmaker odds (Pinnacle via OddsPapi). De-vigged sniff probabilities for home win, shared nap, and away win from bookmaker odds.
Model Confidence Cat Confidence
Strength of the strongest 1/X/2 market signal. High when one outcome dominates; low when the market is split. How loudly the market purrs toward one outcome. High when one side dominates; low when the odds are suspiciously balanced.
Top score distribution Top nap outcomes
Five most likely exact scores from the Poisson model calibrated to market odds — context for the EV pick. Five most likely exact nap scores from the Poisson model — background sniffing for the chosen bowl.
Badges (EV Pick · Market Signal · Confidence) Badges (Tuna Pick · Nap Signal · Purring)
EV Pick — tip optimizes expected points. Market Signal — derived from live odds. High / Low Confidence — market clarity. No odds yet — waiting for bookmaker lines. Tuna Pick — bowl optimizes expected tuna. Nap Signal — derived from live odds. Purring / Suspicious Cat — market clarity. Cat still waiting at the window — no odds yet.
