Why this works Why the cat thinks this works

The agent does not predict football better than the market. It converts market odds into score probabilities and selects the result with the highest expected Kicktipp points. The cat does not claim to see the future. It simply sniffs the market odds, converts them into score probabilities and chooses the bowl with the most expected tuna.
